Design Portfolio Guidelines

A design portfolio is a document that displays a chronological list of all the work associated with a certain project an engineer has done. It is important to keep a design portfolio because holds proof your ownership over original ideas.

Twine Interactive Fiction

This project is an interactive fictional story made on the platform twine. As the protagonist, players get trapped inside of an IKEA, and their goal is to escape. The game includes many alternative routes and secrets for players to explore.
